1. Greetings

“Hello”

“Hi”

“How are you?”

“Good morning/afternoon/evening”

2. Time & Date

“Time” → Jarvis tells the current time

“Date” → Jarvis tells today’s date

3. Weather & Climate

“Weather in [city]” → Jarvis tells temperature and climate

“Climate in [city]” → Jarvis tells climate description

“Temperature in [city]” or “Temp” → Jarvis tells temperature

4. Search & Knowledge

“Search [query]” → Jarvis opens Google search and reads summary

“Define [query]” → Jarvis reads definition from Wikipedia

“What is [query]” → Wikipedia summary

“Who is [person]” → Wikipedia summary

5. Website Navigation

“Open [website]” → Opens website in a new tab

“Open URL [url]” → Opens specific URL in a new tab

6. Music

“Play music” → Opens top hits on YouTube

“Play [song name]” → Opens YouTube search for the song

7. Jokes

“Tell me a joke”

“Make me laugh”

8. Recipes

“Recipe for [dish]”

“Banao [dish]”

Available dishes: Shahi Paneer, Butter Chicken, Paneer Tikka (you can add more)

9. Voice Change

“Change voice to girl” / “female”

“Change voice to woman / old woman”

“Change voice to old man”

“Change voice to young man / man”

“Change voice to child / young boy / young girl”

“Change voice to robot”

“Change voice to animal”

10. Polite Responses

“Sorry” / “Apologize” → Jarvis replies “It’s ok sir, no worries. You’re welcome!”

“Thank you” → Jarvis replies “You’re welcome sir!”

“Shut up” → Jarvis replies “Sorry sir!”
